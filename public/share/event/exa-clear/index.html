Dungeon Clear Challenge

June 26 – July 3, 2026 Ends July 4, 2026 00:00 UTC Dragon Dimension Hard

3,000 crowns and 600 meter shop points across 6 roles. 500 crowns and 100 shop points per #1 Best DPS.

  • Melee: 500 crowns + 100 shop points (#1 Best DPS)
  • Ranged: 500 crowns + 100 shop points (#1 Best DPS)
  • Caster: 500 crowns + 100 shop points (#1 Best DPS)
  • Hybrid: 500 crowns + 100 shop points (#1 Best DPS)
  • Tank: 500 crowns + 100 shop points (#1 Best DPS)
  • Healer: 500 crowns + 100 shop points (#1 Best DPS)

Participation rewards

Random draw: one winner per role for 250 crowns each (1,500 crowns total). Role champions are not eligible for their role's draw. Everyone with at least one eligible upload earns 25 meter shop points.

How it works

  1. Run the announced dungeon during the event window on Hard mode.
  2. Record with Odyssey Companion and upload a dungeon party parse to Meter.
  3. Top Best DPS per role on the leaderboard wins crowns and meter shop points.
  4. Random participation draw: 250 crowns per role (role champions excluded); 25 shop points for every eligible participant.
