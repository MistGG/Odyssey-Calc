Participation rewards

Random draw: one winner per role for 50 crowns each (300 crowns total). Everyone with at least one eligible upload earns 25 meter shop points.

How it works

  1. Run the announced dungeon during the event window on Hard mode.
  2. Record with Odyssey Companion and upload a dungeon party parse to Meter.
  3. Top Best DPS per role on the leaderboard wins crowns and meter shop points.
  4. Random participation draw: 50 crowns per role; 25 shop points for every eligible participant.
